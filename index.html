About

The Dadi Institute of Engineering & Technology is proud to be a part of the CSI Membership Program. The membership program has been designed to provide students and faculty members with access to the latest technology, resources, and industry trends. Through this membership, members will have access to a wide range of activities such as workshops, seminars, conferences and other events that are geared towards enhancing their knowledge in the field of computer science. Additionally, members will also receive discounts on various products and services related to computing technology. This membership provides an excellent opportunity for students and faculty members to stay up-to-date with the latest developments in computer science while also connecting them with professionals from around the world.
